🎬 Welcome to Our Movie Website

Hello there, movie lover! 🎬

Welcome to our website!

We are Group 9, a team of four girls who share the same idea (and probably the same struggle): spending too much time deciding what to watch instead of actually watching something.

So yes… this website was born from our very real problem 😌

Inspired by platforms like Letterboxd, we created a space where you can explore movies, track what you’ve watched, and discover new films without overthinking too much.

Because honestly, sometimes choosing a movie is harder than doing homework.

Through this website, you can:

  • Keep track of your movie journey
  • Find recommendations based on your interests
  • Share your thoughts in a simple and fun way

We designed this platform to be easy, enjoyable, and relatable, especially for students like us.

And since we are a team of four girls, you can expect a little bit of chaos, a lot of opinions, and definitely different movie tastes 🎭

But that’s what makes it fun.

So, are you ready to start your movie journey with us?

🍿 Let’s explore, track, and talk about movies together.
